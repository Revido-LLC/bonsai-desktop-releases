Bonsai for Mac

Sync your Bonsai data and chat with it — right from your menubar.

Download for macOS

Universal (Apple Silicon & Intel) · All versions

  1. Open the downloaded .dmg and drag Bonsai to Applications.
  2. This build is unsigned — run once in Terminal: xattr -cr /Applications/Bonsai.app
    (or open it, then System Settings → Privacy & Security → Open Anyway).
  3. Launch Bonsai, pair it with your account, and sign in when prompted.
